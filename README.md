#he Retail Sales Dashboard provides a centralized, real-time visual command center for tracking store performance, product demand, and revenue growth. It transforms complex point-of-sale (POS) transactional data into actionable insights, allowing retail leaders and store managers to make data-driven decisions.Core Features & CapabilitiesReal-time Revenue Tracking: Instantly view daily, weekly, and monthly sales against set targets. Track KPIs like Total Net Revenue, Gross Profit Margin, and Average Order Value (AOV).Product Performance: Identify top-selling items, best-performing categories, and slow-moving products to optimize merchandising.Store & Regional Analysis: Compare sales volume across different physical branches or e-commerce platforms to identify high-performing locations.
